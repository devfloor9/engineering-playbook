## 0. Pre-Demo Checklist
| ✅ | Top-5 response under 1 minute / 5-BU data live / OPENPOINT statistics / cold chain live |

## 1. Opening (3 min)
> "A working demo that integrates Uni-President's **5 BUs (7-Eleven · Carrefour · Starbucks · Donut · KFC) + Uni-President Food manufacturing + Transnet cold chain** into a single Knowledge Graph, used by 5 departments through tailored views. Not a concept — live data over 5K OPENPOINT members + 49.5K synthetic + per-BU transactions across 5 BUs + 50K cold-chain logs + 4 external sources."

Four key messages:
1. **Cross-BU OPENPOINT member journey** (S9-U) — the PoC's decisive moment
2. **Own-SKU own-channel sell-through** (S10-U) — manufacturing-distribution vertical integration
3. **Cold-chain SLA + outdoor temperature** (S11-U) — own logistics as a core asset
4. **Department persona switcher** — 5-department views

## 2. Top-5 Demo (20 min)

### Step 1 — S1 Semantic Search (4 min)
- "Summer oolong tea PB SKUs + 7ELE stores"
- Result: SKUs + sell-through display (linked to S10-U)

### Step 2 — S9-U Cross-BU Journey ⭐ (6 min, decisive moment)
- P1 → "Cross-BU usage rate among 12M OPENPOINT members"
- Result: 7ELE→Carrefour 30%, 7ELE→Starbucks 12%, BU-pair matrix
- Individual member 90-day timeline (weekday 7ELE → weekend Carrefour → morning Starbucks)
- Emphasis: **"From 5 BU silos to a unified member view — something no competitor can do"**

### Step 3 — S10-U Own-SKU Sell-Through ⭐ (4 min)
- P5 → "Mai-Hsiang (麥香) oolong tea — own channels vs external"
- Result: 65% own / 35% external + new-SKU shipment recommendation

### Step 4 — S11-U Cold-Chain SLA ⭐ (4 min)
- P5 → "Dairy cold chain during southern heatwave"
- Breach rate 28% (vs. 8% baseline) + store-order correction simulation

### Step 5 — S2 Persona Chatbot (4 min)
- "+5pt cross-BU usage campaign" — P1 vs P5

## 3. Governance (3 min)
- cohort_tag · PDPA guards · BU commercial-information segregation · cold-chain pricing protection

## 4. Closing (4 min)
- Wrap-up + next steps (3-week Discovery → 8-week PoC → 12-week operations)
